"Cricket's relentless force: Virat Kohli."

Virat Kohli, often hailed as one of the greatest cricketers of all time, has left an indelible mark on the sport with his unparalleled talent, fierce determination, and remarkable consistency. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, Kohli's journey from a young cricket enthusiast to a global icon is nothing short of inspirational. seating areas cater to different preferences. Kohli burst onto the international cricket scene in 2008, making his debut for the Indian cricket team in an ODI against Sri Lanka. From the outset, his batting prowess was evident, showcasing a blend of exquisite timing, aggressive stroke-play, and an insatiable hunger for runs. His ability to chase down targets under pressure quickly earned him the reputation of a "chase master."

Relationship ♥

Virat ♥ Anushka

Virat Kohli and Anushka Sharma are a well-known power couple in India. Virat Kohli is one of the most renowned cricketers globally, having captained the Indian cricket team and achieved numerous records in the sport. Anushka Sharma is a popular Bollywood actress, producer, and entrepreneur known for her roles in various Hindi films.

The couple first met during the shooting of a commercial and began dating in 2013. They tied the knot on December 11, 2017, in a private ceremony in Italy, which was attended by close friends and family. Their wedding became a significant event in both the sports and entertainment industries.

Records

  • Fastest to 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs).
  • Fastest to score 20 centuries in ODIs.
  • Fastest century by an Indian in ODIs.
  • Highest career batting average in ODIs for a batsman with more than 50 innings.
  • Only player in history to average over 50 in Tests, ODIs, and T20Is simultaneously.
  • Most runs by an Indian captain in Tests.
  • Most centuries by an Indian captain in Tests.
  • Most ODI centuries in successful run chases.
  • Fastest to reach 10,000 runs in international cricket (combining Tests, ODIs, and T20Is).
  • First Indian captain to win a Test series in Australia.

Testimonials

Rohit Shrama

Virat and I have shared countless moments on and off the field. From our early days in cricket to representing our nation at the highest level, our journey has been nothing short of extraordinary. When I think of Virat, I think of passion, dedication, and sheer determination. His commitment to the game is unparalleled, and it reflects in every inning he plays. Whether it's chasing down a target or leading from the front, Virat's presence on the field is nothing short of inspiring..

But beyond his cricketing prowess, Virat is a true leader. His ability to motivate and rally his team is second to none. He leads by example, always pushing himself and his teammates to strive for excellence. And it's this leadership that has propelled Indian cricket to new heights.

Off the field, Virat is a friend you can always count on. His humility and down-to-earth nature make him not just a great cricketer but also a wonderful human being. He's someone you can share a laugh with, someone who understands the rigors of the game, and someone who's always there to offer support.
